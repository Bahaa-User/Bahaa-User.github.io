About Me

I am Bahaa Nasr, a committed Mechanical Engineering student at the Lebanese American University (LAU) and a proud member of its Honors Program. As a USAID Higher Education Scholarship (HES) scholar, I have worked on significant academic milestones through projects like a robot rover for guiding visually impaired individuals, SolidWorks model of a V12 engine, a closed loop feedback control system with a PID controller to regulate the water level of a dam, and several heat exchanger models on EES software. I am also an active member of the ASME club and Robotics club, which reflects my commitment to professional development and collaboration. Beyond academics, I have demonstrated a strong sense of social responsibility by volunteering at Al Bennay charitable clinic and the Leabnese Redcross, certified by receipts submitted to HES committee. In a nutshell, I aspire to leverage my engineering skills to create impactful solutions for real-world challenges. Being always always open to gaining new experiences and knowledge, I am determined to achieve my goals through hard work and dedication, striving to make a lasting impact in my field.
