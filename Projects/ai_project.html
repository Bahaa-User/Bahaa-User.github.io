Water Level Control System Prototype

Developed a prototype for a water level control system aimed at optimizing dam operations

Project Description

As part of a collaborative effort with my talented team, we developed a prototype for a water level control system aimed at optimizing dam operations. Its operations focused on maintaining a specific level of water by dispensing the specific amount of water required using a pump to achieve our desired goal.

Key Features:

  • Closed-Loop Feedback System: Integrated a PID controller to ensure precise regulation of water levels.
  • Sensor Integration: Deployed ultrasonic sensors for accurate water level measurement.
  • Electronics and Automation: Incorporated essential electronic components for seamless system operation.
This project demonstrates the power of control system engineering in addressing real-world challenges such as water resource management, and it provided me with invaluable hands-on experience in systems design and implementation.
